 About MoGoTools
  • When I played a certain gacha game, I used a web app to track my collection. This web app and its amazing filter/sort system is what inspired me to create MoGoTools.
 What MoGoTools does:
  • keeps track of stickers you currently have and don't have
  • has a filter & sort system that allows you to quickly search for stickers by name, set, rarity, etc.
  • a snapshot feature that lets you download the current selection of stickers as an image for you to show to your friends and fellow players
  • a trade post generator for you to copy to groups and servers without having to write a list every time
  • an exportable user data so you can import and export it whenever and wherever you like without registering or log into your account. We do not collect any user data.
What MoGoTools does NOT do:
  • automatically updates your album progress; you will need to manually update the quantity of stickers on your own
  • support linking with your in-game account, but you can enter your IGN name and invite link manually to be stored alongside your sticker data
Left click or press the sticker, where the artwork is located.
MoGoTools supports two filter modes: AND and OR.
  • AND mode: Stickers that match ALL filter conditions will be displayed in the board.
  • OR mode: Stickers that match at least ONE of the filter conditions will be displayed in the board.
Make use of both filter modes to keep track of your album, create snapshots and generate trade posts!
Each filter button in MoGoTools has three filter states. Clicking the button will change it in this order: none (default) > include (GREEN) > exclude (RED).
  • INCLUDE: Stickers must satisfy this filter.
  • EXCLUDE: Stickers must not satisfy this filter.
An example, if the current filter is "Obtained AND NOT 5-star", it means only stickers that you already obtained and at the same time not 5-star will be shown in the board.
However, if the filter becomes "Obtained OR NOT 5-star", that means stickers that are either obtained or is a 5-star will be displayed.
